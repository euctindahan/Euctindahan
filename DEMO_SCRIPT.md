# 🎙️ Tindahang Envergista: Official Demo Script

This script is designed for a live demonstration. It follows a chronological narrative through the three main user roles.

---

## 🏁 Part 1: Opening (1 Minute)
**[Action: Stand in front of the audience, Landing Page visible on screen]**

"Good day, everyone! I am [Your Name], and today I am excited to show you **Tindahang Envergista**—the first-ever dedicated digital marketplace built specifically for the community of Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation. 

Our mission is simple: to provide student entrepreneurs with a professional platform to showcase their crafts while ensuring every transaction on campus is safe, verified, and efficient. Let’s dive into our first perspective: The Student Buyer."

---

## 🛒 Part 2: Persona 1 - The Customer (2 Minutes)
**[Action: Scroll through the Product Grid]**

"Imagine I am a student looking for a handmade gift for a friend. As I browse the marketplace, you’ll notice a clean, 'premium hardware' design. Every product you see here is made or sold by a fellow Envergista."

**[Action: Click on a specific product]**

"I’ll click on this 'Handmade Leather Journal.' Notice the **'Verified Seller'** checkmark next to the name. This gives me peace of mind knowing the school admin has already audited this seller's identity. I can read the description—which, by the way, was assisted by AI—and check if it’s still in stock."

**[Action: Open the Chat interface]**

"I have a quick question about the color, so I’ll use our **Real-time Chat**. I’ll type: *'Hi! Is the brown version still available?'* The seller receives this instantly, and we can coordinate a meet-up right here on campus."

**[Action: Click 'Add to Cart' and proceed to 'Checkout']**

"Now, I’m ready to buy. I’ll choose **GCash** as my payment method. Security is our priority, so the system requires me to upload a **Screenshot of my payment**. I’ll attach my receipt here, click 'Place Order,' and I’m done! My order is now safely logged in the system."

---

## 📈 Part 3: Persona 2 - The Seller (2 Minutes)
**[Action: Switch to the Seller Dashboard view]**

"Now, let’s see what happens on the other side. I am now the Student Seller. When I log in, I don’t just see orders; I see my business growth. Our **Seller Dashboard** features dynamic charts showing my weekly revenue and sales trends. This isn't just a shop; it’s a business tool."

**[Action: Navigate to 'My Products' and click 'Add Product']**

"Let’s say I have a new item to sell. I’ll upload the photo and type a basic description. Here is where it gets smart: I’ll click **'Generate with AI'**. Our integration with **Google Gemini** automatically crafts a professional, appealing description for me."

**[Action: Navigate to 'Order Management' and view the order from earlier]**

"I just received a notification for that Journal order we placed earlier. I can see the customer's **GCash receipt** right here. It looks legitimate, so I’ll update the status to **'Preparing'**. The customer gets a notification instantly. No more messy text messages or lost orders."

---

## ⚖️ Part 4: Persona 3 - The Admin (3 Minutes)
**[Action: Switch to the Admin Dashboard]**

"Finally, we have the most important role: The University Administrator. This is what keeps our campus safe. As an Admin, I oversee the entire ecosystem."

**[Action: Navigate to 'Verification Requests']**

"Here, I can see a list of students who want to become sellers. I’ll check this applicant's **Student ID upload**. It matches our records, so I’ll click **'Approve'**. They are now authorized to sell on the platform. This layer of human verification is what makes us different from social media marketplaces."

**[Action: Navigate to 'Orders' or 'Global Transactions' tab]**

"Transparency is paramount in campus commerce. As an Admin, I have **Full Order Supervision.** I can monitor every transaction on the platform. If there is a dispute between a buyer and a seller, I can see the exact time the order was placed and view the payment receipt. This high-level oversight ensures that both parties are held accountable."

**[Action: Navigate to 'Reports' section]**

"Data is our most valuable asset. The Control Center features a **Reporting Engine.** I can generate system-wide reports on sales volume, top-performing categories, and seller growth. These reports provide the university with actionable data for reporting to school boards or planning student entrepreneurship programs."

**[Action: Navigate to 'System Settings']**

"Admins also control the platform's economics. I can adjust the **Listing Tax** here—this 5% fee ensures the foundation can maintain the servers. I can also toggle **Maintenance Mode** during summer breaks. Everything is under my control, ensuring the university guidelines are always followed."

---

## 🏁 Part 5: Closing (30 Seconds)
**[Action: Return to the Landing Page]**

"Tindahang Envergista is a bridge between campus creativity and professional commerce. By combining **React 19**, **Firebase Real-time logic**, and **Gemini AI**, we’ve built a tool that prepares students for the digital economy. 

Thank you for your time, and I am now open for any questions!"
